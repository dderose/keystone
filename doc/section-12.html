12.0 :: Campaign Unit

The Campaign Unit represents a summary of a campaign and is generally used on the Toolkit dashboard and Tookkit campaigns pages. There are two variations: a hero unit and a more collapse summary unit which is the default.

By default, this component is responsive. Under 481px it collapse to two rows with the title on top and the stats and actions dropdown in the second row.

From 481px to 768px the unit will shift to be side by side. By convention only two stats, at most, should be visible at this breakpoint. Use the classes b1-u-hide and b2-u-showTableCell as indicated below to hide columns. Each stat column with be expand to take up the amount of space the content inside of it needs and the title column will expand to consume to remaining space. Additionally, the dropdown shall lose the label and collapse to only show a caret.

From 769px and up all stats will be shown. Additionally, the stats columns will be converted to a minimum width to ensure that stats line up vertically on the page between each unit.

Note that the first (leftmost) stats column requires the classes u-paddingLeftFlush and b1-u-paddingLeft at all times.
