14.0 :: Stat

The Stat component displays a statistic with a caption.

25 opens

.Stat--fixedWidth

Give the Stat a min-width of 112px.

25 opens

<p class="Stat Stat--fixedWidth"><a href="#" class="Stat-data" data-qe-id="{{campaign.stat}}">25</a> opens</p>

.Stat--rwd

Left aligns text on resolutions below 769px and right aligns text on all other resolutions.

25 opens

<p class="Stat Stat--rwd"><a href="#" class="Stat-data" data-qe-id="{{campaign.stat}}">25</a> opens</p>
